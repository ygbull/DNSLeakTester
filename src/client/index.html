Leak Tester

Network privacy diagnostic

One-click scan to detect DNS leaks, WebRTC IP exposure, TLS fingerprint uniqueness, and browser entropy.

Takes about 10 seconds. No data is stored.

What does this tool check?

When you use a VPN, your web traffic should be encrypted and routed through the VPN tunnel. A DNS leak occurs when your DNS queries bypass the tunnel and go directly to your ISP's resolver, revealing which websites you visit.

This tool checks for DNS leaks, WebRTC IP exposure, TLS fingerprint uniqueness, and browser fingerprint entropy to give you an overall network privacy grade.
